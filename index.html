Screens Description

Station selection

  1. Tocs - This allows you to select a Train Operating company. For most users there will only be one option in this list, however if you are part of a TOC Group, you will have the option to select multiple TOCS. To clear this filter, press the red 'x' icon.
  2. Arr/Dep - This allows you to display either Services arriving at the selected station, or Departing from the selected station, or both.
  3. Select Days -Here you can select a day of the week, or multiple days of the week. To clear this filter, press the red 'x' icon.
  4. Time Filter - Here you can select the AM or PM Peak times.
  5. Timetable - This filter will automatically populate the timetable you select into the date range filter, to allow you to easily switch between timetables.
  6. Stop at/passes - Here you can filter your results to show only Services that pass through select stations or passing points (such as junctions).
  7. Does not stop at/passes - Here you can filter your results to not show Services that pass through select stations or passing points (such as junctions).
  8. Origin Station - Select the station from where the service starts its journey
  9. Destination Station - Select the station where the service ended its journey
  10. Date Range - Select the date range your search will be based on
  11. Excluded Dates - Select the dates that you want to exclude from the search
  12. Toggle this to show formations on hover
  13. Train Load -
  14. Use this to export results in .csv format

FAQ

Why the train load is take long to show results?

    If you have longer date range selected the train load can take longer to load. For faster results try to use shorter date range.

How can i download data?

    By clicking the download icon from the left corner of the station screen and count screen you can download data shown on that search

Why I do not see any stations on station screen?

    Make sure you have selected the correct timetable

I have a problem that is not on FAQ list
